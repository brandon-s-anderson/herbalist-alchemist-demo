Regulatory Compliance — Automated

Prop 65, FDA & Hazmat DOT

⚠️ California Prop 65

Products tagged "CA Prop 65" show warnings on the product page automatically. At checkout, extension checks shipping state — California + flagged product = warning displayed.

🚛 Hazmat DOT Shipping

Three automated layers: product page notice, Shopify Flow flags hazmat orders + notifies fulfillment, order rules auto-split at 194 oz limit.

💊 FDA Disclaimers

"These statements have not been evaluated by the FDA" — stored as product metafield, rendered on every product page by the theme. One setup, 250 products.

DOT Hazmat — How It Works
